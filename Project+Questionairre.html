  1. What is the goal of the game?

The Prince needs to cross different obstacles, defeating each level, to finally meet the boss character who kidnaps the princess.



  1. Write a brief story of your game?

The prince and the princess are separated when a dragon, kidnaps the princess. The prince now must come across different obstacles in different biomes to find the dragon’s lair, and to save the kidnapped princess.

How do you plan to make your game engaging?

There will be different obstacles to attack the prince, and the prince can defeat the obstacle by throwing an item on it. This “item” can be collected during different areas in the game. He is left with only 3 lives, and once each life is depleted, it will take 10 mins for the lives to replenish. The final boss level is when the prince comes face to face with the princess kidnapper, the dragon, and attempts to rescue the princess by throwing the dragons weakness, an ice ball, which can be found at the middle and end of each level. The prince finally defeats the dragon and they both go home, living “happily ever after”.
